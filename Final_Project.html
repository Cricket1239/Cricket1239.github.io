Gem Quest is a short adventure and lightweight puzzle game. You play as a theif girl who's broken into a castle to secure some treasure.

The game itself is quite simple, as it was designed to be easily expanded upon. Use the left and right arrow keys to move the theif.
You'll find your path blocked by a creature, luckily for you somebody left a sword nearby. Move close to it and click on it to obtain
the sword! Now you're able to get past that monster. Now make your way through the castle and obtain that treasure!

If you're stumped on what to do next, try traveling to the edge of the screen (To the right for screen one and to the left for screen two).
You're able to transition between both screens. And remember, some objects will only work when you're near them. Now don't forget to escape
through the gate when you've claimed your prize!
